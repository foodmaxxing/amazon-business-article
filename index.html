If you're a business owner — whether you run a restaurant, a home service company, an e-commerce store, or even a small office — you're probably already buying supplies on Amazon. But you're almost certainly paying more than you need to.


Amazon Business is a free, separate account designed specifically for companies and organizations. It unlocks bulk discounts, business-only pricing, flexible payment options, and powerful spending controls that regular Amazon accounts simply don't offer. The best part? It costs nothing to get started.

The Benefits

6 Reasons to Sign Up for Amazon Business Today

💰

Business-Only Pricing & Bulk Discounts

Amazon Business unlocks pricing that regular shoppers never see. You can request quantity discounts from sellers, access tiered pricing on bulk orders, and find products specifically listed for business buyers at lower per-unit costs.

→ Save more the more you buy
📄

Pay by Invoice — Buy Now, Pay Later (No Interest)

Eligible businesses get access to Pay by Invoice: a built-in line of credit with standard 30-day payment terms, no interest, and no upfront fees. It helps smooth out cash flow so you're not paying out of pocket every time you need to restock. Business Prime members can extend those terms to 45 or even 60 days.

→ Better cash flow, zero interest
📊

Spending Analytics & Visibility Dashboards

Amazon Business gives you a visual dashboard to track what your company is buying, who's buying it, and where the money is going. Spot trends, identify waste, and make smarter budget decisions — all from one central hub. Order history reports are downloadable for bookkeeping too.

→ Understand your spend at a glance
👥

Multi-User Accounts with Approval Controls

Add employees to your account and set exactly what they can buy, what they can spend, and which payment methods they can use. You can create approval workflows so big purchases need sign-off before they go through. No more surprise orders on the company card.

→ Control purchasing across your whole team
🚫

Tax Exemption Made Easy

Qualifying businesses and organizations can apply for tax-exempt status directly through Amazon's Tax Exemption Program. Once approved, eligible purchases are automatically processed without sales tax — saving you money on every qualifying order without any manual effort.

→ Stop overpaying on sales tax
🔁

Subscribe & Save + Easy Reordering

For the things you order regularly — paper, cleaning supplies, coffee, batteries — Subscribe & Save lets you set automatic deliveries and save up to 15% on select products. No minimum order, no commitment. Combine this with bulk pricing and you'll consistently beat what you'd pay at Costco or Staples on many items.

→ Automate your recurring purchases
